How to use it :

You can use our Template!

After filling out the Table, simply save it and upload it!

Not sure how to fill out our template? Here are some Tips and Examples:

  • The first two columns "Question" and "Answer" are mandatory!
  • if have not entered an alternative answer, this question will give you a Prompt field where you can enter your answer to this question.
  • If you enter alternative answers for a question, it will become a Multiple Choice Question in a Quiz. This means that you will have to choose the correct answer from this choices.
  • Alternative answers must be seperated by comma
  • There are 3 choices for Difficulty: Easy, Medium and Hard! If you do not set a difficulty, it will be automatically set to Medium.
